Logistics

Travel and Lodging

Travel

Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA) is the closest airport to the wedding activities. It is 16 miles from downtown Seattle.

You can rent a car, catch a Lyft or Uber or take the Link Light Rail (35-40 mins to downtown for $3) directly from the terminal to get to Seattle and surrounding areas.

Seattle has great public transportation, so renting a car isn’t crucial.

Lodging

All of the wedding activities will take place near downtown Seattle and Belltown. Hotel room block information is coming soon.

Airbnb is also popular in Seattle. There are many options available in downtown Seattle starting at $110/night and up.
